Business Overview

Founded by Dr Aperna Volluru in 2007, the Ritzy group is a global enterprise, headquartered in Italy, comprising 4 companies across Seven verticals. The group operates in more than 10 countries across two continents, offering 21st century education and facility solutions to promote deep learning and fostering the success skills of critical thinking, problem solving, collaboration and self- management essential in the workplace of tomorrow.
